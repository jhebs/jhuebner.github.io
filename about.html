I'm Jennifer & this is a bit about me.

What introduced you to design?

.......

The design process was introduced to me in a position when my company wanted to integrate the design process with their agile sprints. The company allowed employees to attend seminars and complete online courses. I was lucky enough to attend a seminar hosted by our local design team across the hall from my desk. And it was that seminar that changed my life. I was a kid in a candy store watching the lead developer tell us the story of the design process. It was that day that I wanted to learn as much as possible about the design process and become a designer. And so I did!

I signed up for a 9-month UX apprenticeship with Bloc. And can be found listening to the latest UX podcast, following UX designers on Twitter and reading as many articles as I can. When it comes to visual design, I like taking inspiration from Dribbble, Behance, and Pinterest. I like to practice my visual design skills in Figma.

Why is design important to you?

.......

Design is important to me because the design is intentional. Design is present and embedded in the world around us, from the buildings that we live in, the cars we drive, and the chairs that we sit on. Design thinking is used in every one of these examples. At a high level, design thinking is a problem-solving process with five distinct elements:

  • Define the problem
  • Create Options
  • Refine & Iterate
  • Repeat
  • Execute

As a designer, these have been the fundamental principals I live by. I am excited for the next step in my career and to be apart of the design process in a professional setting.

How can you use design to make a difference?

.......

With the ever-growing emergence of technology, there is a huge need for accessibility. It's important to create diverse experiences for all users. People who interact with technology are diverse, with a wide variety of characteristics and contexts. Creating accessible experiences while solving a problem is important to me as a designer. I will leverage my business background, love for data, and empathy to create accessible and meaningful experiences.
